What is Artificial Intelligence? - Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines. - AI systems can think, learn, and act like humans. Types of Artificial Intelligence: 1. Narrow or Weak AI: Designed to perform a specific task (e.g., Siri, Google Assistant). 2. General or Strong AI: Can perform any intellectual task (e.g., human-like reasoning). 3. Superintelligence: Significantly more intelligent than humans. Key Features of AI: 1. Machine Learning: AI learns from data. 2. Natural Language Processing: AI understands human language. 3. Computer Vision: AI interprets visual data. 4. Robotics: AI interacts with physical environment. 5. Expert Systems: AI makes decisions based on rules. Applications of Artificial Intelligence: 1. Virtual Assistants: Siri, Google Assistant, Alexa. 2. Image Recognition: Self-driving cars, facial recognition. 3. Predictive Maintenance: Industrial equipment monitoring. 4. Chatbots: Customer service, language translation. 5. Healthcare: Diagnosis, personalized medicine. 6. Finance: Trading, risk analysis. 7. Education: Personalized learning. AI Techniques: 1. Deep Learning: Neural networks. 2. Decision Trees: Tree-like models. 3. Random Forest: Ensemble learning. 4. Support Vector Machines: Classification. 5. Genetic Algorithms: Optimization.
